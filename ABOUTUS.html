ABOUT US


This digital humanities project is an assignment for AQC7015,
aiming to create a digital timeline of Su Shi's life and works. It is completed by the following members:

MIAO CHUYAN 23077537
LIU XI 23076177
CHANG WENJIE 24059552
SHI MUGE 23097276
MACHEN 23092843

The project serves as a comprehensive platform to collect, organize, and present key events in Su Shi's life,
including his literary creations, political experiences, and life trajectories.
It aims to provide users with convenient access to detailed data on his poems' creation backgrounds,
official career changes, and ideological evolution, promoting the inheritance and
dissemination of Song Dynasty culture and Su Shi's humanistic spirit.
Through this project, we hope to demonstrate our understanding and application of relevant theories and technologies in the AQC7015 course,
while making contributions to the digitalization and modern interpretation of traditional Chinese literary heritage.
